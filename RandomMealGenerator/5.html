Soba

Soba ( Japanese :蕎麦, そば) is a Japanese dish in which noodles made from buckwheat flour are dipped in hot broth or cold soy sauce with radish , green onion, and wasabi . Buckwheat began to be made into noodles in the late 16th and early 17th centuries.

Category : rice
Area : Korea

Ingredients

- beef stock
- soy source
