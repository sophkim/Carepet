Bulgogi

Bulgogi ( English : Bulgogi ) is a traditional Korean meat dish made by marinating beef in seasoning and adding vegetables . Those made with pork are called pork bulgogi. For roasting, parts such as tenderloin or sirloin are most often used because the meat, which has little connective tissue and is interspersed with fat, is tasty and tender .

Category : Beef
Area : Korea

- beef
- sugar
